# GuerrillaTech Academy Organizational Design

## Overview
This document outlines the organizational structure of the GuerrillaTech Academy, a strategic ecosystem integrating STEM education, AI innovation, and ethical entrepreneurship.

## Key Divisions
1. Command Division
2. Ohio Tech Academy Division
3. SallirreugTech Division
4. Research and Ethics Division

## Strategic Purpose
Provide strategic direction, ensure ethical compliance, and integrate education, innovation, and entrepreneurship across all entities.

- Admiralty (Strategic Command)

- Non-Commissioned Officers (NCOs)
- Enlisted Personnel

- Equitable STEM Education
- Innovation and Entrepreneurship
- Community Outreach
